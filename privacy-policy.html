1. Introduction & Business Nature

Binapani Automation ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website or services.

This policy is compliant with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act).

⚠️ IMPORTANT: B2B Commercial Entity Only

Unlike "Binapani: The Learning App," Binapani Automation is a strictly B2B industrial trading and technical service company. Our services are designed exclusively for commercial industrial entities.

We do NOT:

  • Collect data from minors or individuals under 18 years of age
  • Provide educational services or operate as a learning platform
  • Handle student data, academic records, or education-related information
  • Target consumers or retail customers

Target Audience: Factory owners, industrial plant managers, automation engineers, OEMs, and B2B procurement professionals only.

2. Information We Collect (B2B Commercial Data)

We collect the following types of commercial business information:

  • Business Contact Information: Contact person name, job title, company name, business email, business phone number
  • Commercial Data: GST number, company registration details, purchase order information
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information (collected via website analytics)
  • Business Inquiry Data: Project specifications, technical requirements, equipment models needed

Lead Generation Purpose: Information collected is used solely for generating quotations for industrial automation parts, equipment, and technical services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your commercial business data to:

  • Process business inquiries and generate quotations
  • Provide technical support and engineering services
  • Send product availability updates and technical documentation
  • Fulfill orders and coordinate deliveries
  • Comply with GST, Income Tax, and other business legal requirements
  • Improve our B2B services and website functionality

4. Data Sharing & Third-Party Processors

We do NOT sell your business information. We may share data with:

  • Product suppliers: Only as necessary to fulfill orders (company name, delivery address)
  • Government authorities: As required by law (GST returns, tax audits)

No CRM or Marketing Automation: Currently, we do not use third-party CRM systems. Data is stored locally and in business email accounts.

Remarketing/Retargeting: We do NOT currently use Google Ads remarketing or retargeting technologies. If we implement these in the future, this policy will be updated with explicit consent mechanisms.
